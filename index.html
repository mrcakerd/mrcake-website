El Arte Detrás
de Cada Cake

I didn't plan to become a cake designer. I planned to be an animator — then a chef — and somewhere between a culinary degree, years leading kitchens in hotels and restaurants, and a career managing operations for events companies, I found the thing I was always meant to do.

The turning point? Watching Duff Goldman build edible sculptures on TV and thinking: that's it. That's what I want to make.

My name is Frank González. I'm a classically trained chef and formally trained cake designer and sugar artist based in Santo Domingo. I hold a ServSafe food safety certification and have competed on Food Network Canada's The Big Bake — but what truly shapes my work is a career built inside professional kitchens and the discipline that comes with it.

Most recently, Mr. Cake was among the 22 sugar artists selected to participate in RD Bridal Week 2026 — the Caribbean's most influential bridal platform — where cake design was elevated to fine art. The closing event, The Cake Party at Kimpton Las Mercedes, was covered by Vogue México & Latinoamérica.

At Mr. Cake, every piece starts with a conversation, not a catalog. I design digitally in Procreate before a single gram of sugar is used — so what you approve is exactly what arrives at your venue.

I work in Spanish and English — because your wedding deserves a designer who speaks your language.

Every cake at Mr. Cake is built through a collaborative process. No surprises, no shortcuts. Cada cake en Mr. Cake se construye a través de un proceso colaborativo. Sin sorpresas, sin atajos.

01
Initial Consultation Consulta Inicial

Tell us about your wedding — your vision, date, venue, style and number of guests. This conversation is where everything begins. Cuéntanos sobre tu boda — tu visión, fecha, venue, estilo y número de invitados. Esta conversación es el punto de partida de todo.

02
Custom Quote Cotización Personalizada

Based on your consultation, we prepare a proposal designed specifically for your wedding. Nothing generic, nothing off-the-shelf. Basada en tu consulta, preparamos una propuesta a la medida de tu boda. Nada genérico, nada estándar.

03
Reservation & Tasting Box Reserva & Tasting Box

A 50% deposit confirms your date — and we send you your complimentary Tasting Box so you choose your flavors with complete confidence. Con el 50% confirmamos tu fecha — y te enviamos tu Tasting Box de regalo para que elijas tus sabores con certeza.

04
Digital Design Diseño en Procreate

We create the digital design of your cake together in Procreate. We don't produce a single element without your approval. Creamos el diseño digital de tu cake juntos en Procreate. No producimos nada sin tu aprobación.

05
Production Producción

Your cake is crafted under ServSafe food safety standards. Some designs require up to a month of pre-production — because the level of detail we deliver cannot be rushed. Elaboramos tu cake bajo estándares ServSafe. Algunos diseños requieren hasta un mes de preproducción — porque el nivel de detalle que entregamos no se apresura.

06
Final Payment & Delivery Pago Final & Entrega

The remaining balance is due 15 days before your wedding. Transport and installation at your venue are included — your cake arrives in our hands, and no one else's. El saldo restante se liquida 15 días antes de tu boda. El transporte e instalación están incluidos — tu cake llega en nuestras manos, y en las de nadie más.

FAQ

Todo lo que necesitas saber

How far in advance should I book? ¿Con cuánta anticipación debo reservar? +
We recommend booking at least 3 months in advance to guarantee your date. For peak season weddings, the earlier the better. Recomendamos reservar con al menos 3 meses de anticipación. En temporada alta, entre más antes mejor.
Can I see the design before production? ¿Puedo ver el diseño antes de producir? +
Always. Every cake is designed digitally in Procreate and we don't begin production until you've approved every detail. Siempre. Cada cake se diseña digitalmente en Procreate y no comenzamos producción hasta que apruebes cada detalle.
What frostings do you work with? ¿Con qué coberturas trabajan? +
We work with white ganache, fondant and Swiss buttercream — each with distinct characteristics depending on your design and venue. We'll guide you to the best option. Trabajamos con ganache blanco, fondant y buttercream suizo — cada uno con características distintas. Te asesoramos sobre cuál es la mejor opción para tu boda.
Can each tier have a different flavor? ¿Cada piso puede tener diferente sabor? +
Yes — each tier can have a different cake and filling combination, so every guest finds their favorite. Sí — cada piso puede tener un bizcocho y relleno diferente. Así cada invitado encuentra su favorito.
Do you offer vegan & gluten-free options? ¿Tienen opciones veganas y sin gluten? +
Yes. Let us know at the time of your quote and we'll accommodate your needs. Sí. Consúltanos al momento de tu cotización y nos adaptamos a tus necesidades.
What is a display cake? ¿Qué es un cake escenográfico? +
A display cake is visually identical to your real cake but designed to hold its shape for hours — ideal for beach weddings, outdoor ceremonies or long events. It's paired with a separate serving cake for the cutting. Es un cake visualmente idéntico al real pero diseñado para lucir horas sin perder su forma — ideal para bodas en playa, exteriores o eventos largos. Se complementa con un cake de servicio para el corte.
Do you deliver outside Santo Domingo? ¿Entregan fuera de Santo Domingo? +
Yes — we deliver across the Dominican Republic. Punta Cana, La Romana, Las Terrenas, Jarabacoa and beyond. Transport and installation are always handled by us. Sí — entregamos en toda República Dominicana. Punta Cana, La Romana, Las Terrenas, Jarabacoa y más. El transporte e instalación siempre corren por nuestra cuenta.
Can you replicate a cake I found online? ¿Pueden replicar un cake que encontré? +
We can draw inspiration from references you bring, but every Mr. Cake piece is an original. What we guarantee is that the result will be at the level of your vision — or beyond it. Podemos inspirarnos en referencias que tengas, pero cada pieza de Mr. Cake es original. Lo que garantizamos es que el resultado va a estar a la altura de tu visión — o superarla.
